Graphic designer and researcher from Bari (Puglia, Italy). He holds a bachelor’s degree in Industrial Design at Polytechnic of Bari and a master’s degree in Communication Design at Iuav University of Venice.

His practice explores typography in its form and structure, information and editorial design and all the ways they interpolate each other within and without visual systems. His research is oriented also towards design histories, open tools and learning collective ecosystems outside the institutional walls.
